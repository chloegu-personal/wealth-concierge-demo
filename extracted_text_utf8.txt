Anatomy of a Perfect Prompt: The Fintech UI
Key Insight: Explicitly mentioning specific libraries (Chart.js), color codes, and layout constraints guarantees production-ready UI rather than generic wireframes.

--- Slide 25 ---
Anatomy of a Perfect Prompt: Backend & Architecture
Key Insight: Listing specific architectural constrains (UUIDs, caching, normalization) forces the AI to output robust, scalable backend code instead of fragile scripts.
